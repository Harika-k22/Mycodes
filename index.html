WHAT IS MORSE CODE?

Morse code is an alphabet or code in which letters are represented by combinations of long and short light or sound signals. One of the Morse code systems was invented in the United States by American artist and inventor Samuel F.B. Morse during the 1830s for electrical telegraphy.
